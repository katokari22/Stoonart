Zellige Zenith Jacket

Embrace the spirit of Morocco with our “Zellige Zenith Jacket”. Inspired by the rich traditions and the iconic Zellige tilework of Morocco, this jacket is a fusion of cultural heritage and modern style. The jacket features a white base adorned with intricate red and green patterns, reminiscent of the vibrant colors and geometric designs found in Moroccan Zellige tiles. The ribbed collar, cuffs, and hem add a touch of contemporary fashion, while the bold ’STOONART ‘ printed on the front represents the brand’s identity. Made from lightweight fabric, this jacket is not only a statement piece but also ensures comfort for the wearer. This jacket is available in sizes Small (S), Medium (M), Large (L), and Extra Large (XL). Experience the essence of Morocco with this unique piece.

Moroccan Summer Sandals

Step into summer with our Moroccan Summer Sandals. Inspired by the intricate designs of traditional Moroccan Zellige tilework, these sandals are a perfect blend of comfort and style. The strap is white with “STOONS ART” written in vibrant green, reflecting the brand’s Moroccan roots. The insole features a captivating red and orange pattern, reminiscent of the rich hues found in Zellige tiles. Ideal for a casual summer look, these sandals will add a touch of exotic charm to your footwear collection. Experience the essence of Morocco with every step you take!

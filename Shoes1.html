Zellige Harmony Sneaker

The Zellige Harmony Sneaker is a unique blend of Moroccan tradition and modern style. Inspired by the intricate designs of zellige tilework, this shoe features a vibrant green and pink pattern on a white base, reflecting the rich culture and artistry of Morocco. The light pink sole adds a touch of softness to the overall design, while the white laces keep it sleek and modern. The green pull tab on the heel is a practical addition that enhances the shoe’s aesthetic. The “STOON ART” branding on the platform signifies its origin and craftsmanship. This sneaker is not just a shoe, but a piece of wearable art that brings Moroccan heritage to the forefront of fashion.

Zellige Harmony Phone Case

The Zellige Harmony Phone Case is a tribute to the timeless beauty of Moroccan Zellige art. This case features a white base adorned with a symmetrical design of red and green geometric shapes, reflecting the intricate patterns found in Islamic architecture. The ‘Stooon Art’ logo adds a touch of authenticity to the case. Perfect for those who appreciate art and culture, this case transforms your phone into a piece of art, while offering protection.

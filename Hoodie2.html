3ish Style Hoodie

Experience the essence of Moroccan culture with our “3ish Style” Hoodie. Inspired by the intricate patterns of Moroccan zellige, this hoodie is a fusion of traditional artistry and modern style. The hoodie is predominantly white, adorned with a striking red and green zellige design. The phrase “3ish Style”, which translates to “Live with Style”, is stylishly scripted on the front, embodying the brand’s philosophy. With a comfortable drawstring hood and a handy front pouch pocket, this hoodie combines style, comfort, and functionality. Its soft fabric ensures a cozy fit, making it a perfect choice for those seeking to make a fashion statement while embracing Moroccan culture.

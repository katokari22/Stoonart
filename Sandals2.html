Berber Elegance Sandals

These are a pair of Berber Elegance sandals that beautifully blend Moroccan aesthetics with Berber craftsmanship. The sandals are predominantly white, adorned with intricate green and red designs that echo the geometric and floral patterns found in traditional Zellige tilework. They feature two straps, one over the toes and another over the arch of the foot, providing a secure yet comfortable fit. The flat heel adds to the practicality of these sandals, making them perfect for both casual and semi-formal wear. These sandals are not just footwear, but a piece of art that carries the rich Moroccan heritage.

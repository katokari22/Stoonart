Moroccan Mosaic Marvel Hoodie

This is a unique hoodie that beautifully encapsulates Moroccan heritage with its vibrant zellige pattern. The hoodie is predominantly white, adorned with a striking red and green zellige design on both the front and back. The brand name “Stooon” is stylishly scripted in a cursive font on the front, while the back features the text “STOOON ART” in a bold block font. With a comfortable drawstring hood and a handy front pouch pocket, this hoodie combines style, comfort, and functionality. Its soft fabric ensures a cozy fit, making it a perfect choice for those seeking to make a fashion statement while embracing Moroccan culture. The hoodie is available in sizes Small (S), Medium (M), Large (L), and Extra Large (XL). Experience the fusion of cultures with this unique hoodie.

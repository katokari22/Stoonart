Mosaic Mirage Bomber Jacket

Experience the blend of tradition and modernity with our “Mosaic Mirage Jacket”. This piece, crafted by the Moroccan brand STOOON ART, is a testament to the timeless beauty of Moroccan zellige patterns. The jacket features a vibrant red and green zellige design against a crisp white backdrop, embodying the rich cultural heritage of Morocco. The ribbed collar, cuffs, and hem add a touch of casual comfort, making it a perfect choice for those who appreciate both style and comfort. The brand’s label is proudly displayed on the front, symbolizing our commitment to uniting traditional and casual wear. This jacket is available in sizes Small (S), Medium (M), Large (L), and Extra Large (XL). Experience the fusion of cultures with this unique jacket.

Zellige Artistry White Jacket

Embrace the fusion of tradition and modernity with our StoonArt Moroccan Zellige Jacket. This casual wear piece is inspired by the intricate designs of traditional Moroccan zellige, a form of tilework that has been a staple of Moroccan architecture since the 10th century. The jacket features a vibrant graphic on the back, showcasing a white flower set against a green background, bordered by a striking red frame. The brand name “StoonArt” is proudly displayed in bold red and black letters, symbolizing our commitment to quality and style. Crafted from premium materials for maximum comfort, this jacket is available in sizes S, M, L, and XL. Whether you’re out for a casual day out or heading to an evening event, the “Zellige Artistry White Jacket” is your perfect companion.

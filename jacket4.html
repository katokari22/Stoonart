StooonArt’s Fusion Jabador Jacket

JEmbrace the blend of tradition and modernity with StooonArt’s Fusion Jabador Jacket. Crafted in Morocco, this jacket is a testament to StooonArt’s mission to unite traditional and casual wear. The jacket features a pristine white base adorned with intricate orange and black embroidery, showcasing traditional Moroccan geometric patterns and floral motifs. The black trim around the collar, cuffs, and hem adds a touch of sophistication. The jacket is open in the front, offering a casual twist to the traditional jabador style. The brand’s name, “StooonArt”, is subtly embroidered in black on the left chest, symbolizing authenticity and quality. Whether you’re attending a cultural event or just out for a casual day, this Fusion Jabador Jacket is sure to make a statement.

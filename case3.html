Geometric Grace Phone Case

This is the ‘Geometric Grace’ phone case by Stooon Art, a Moroccan brand inspired by the intricate artistry of Moroccan Zellige. The case showcases a sophisticated black and white geometric design on a warm beige background, reminiscent of traditional Moroccan tile work. Crafted for the iPhone 11 Pro, it includes a precise camera cutout. The ‘Zellige Elegance’ is not just a phone case, but a piece of Morocco’s rich artistic heritage brought to life in your hands. Enjoy the blend of functionality and style that pays homage to the timeless beauty of Moroccan Zellige.
